## Magnetic Field on Weyl Semimetals
- $$ H = \vec{p} \cdot \vec{\sigma} $$ $\Rightarrow$
\[ (p_x -e A_x)\sigma_x + (p_y -eA_y)\sigma_y + p_z\sigma-z\].
- Magnetic field along $z$ direction.
- Solve Landau level for $p_z = 0$. 
- $E_n = v_F \sqrt{Bn}$, there is a $n=0$ LL, which is completely polarized  LL.  
- Away from $p_z=0$, we have in general $E_{n} = v_F \sqrt{(Bn)^2 + p_z^2}$.
- length scale $l_B = 1/\sqrt{B}$
