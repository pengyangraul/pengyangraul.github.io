## 1D SPT $\to$ binding $\mathbb{Z}\_2$ charge to $\mathbb{Z}\_2$ domain wall $\to$ condense
IQHE $\to$ $\mathbb{Z} \times \mathbb{Z}$. Duality bosons $\leftrightarrow$ vortices
### Duality Dirac fermions in 2+1D $\to$ QHE $\nu=1/2$, surface states of TI
\[
  H = -J \sum_{i} Z_{i} X_{i+1} Z_{i+2},
\]
where index even means $\sigma$ and index odd means $\mu$. The generators $\mathbb{Z}\_2\times\mathbb{Z}\_2$:
$g_{1} = \prod_{even} X_{i}$ and $g_2 = \prod_{odd} X_i$.
- Periodic BC
$X'\_i = Z_{i-1}X_{i} \times Z_{i+1}$. We then have $H = -J \sum X'\_i$. Each local term commutes with each other.
$\Rigtharrow$ unique ground state with PBC. 
- Open BC : expect degeneracy.
Construct boundary operators $\Sigma_L^Z = Z_{0}$, $\Sigma_L^X = X_0 Z_1$, and $\Sigma_L^Y  = Y_0 Z_1$.
Since $\Sigma_L^Z$ an $\Sigma_L^X$ don't commute, we have at least two-fold degeneracy at the left end.
Also the right end. Thus, we have 4-fold degeneracy.
Generators: $g_{1} = \Sigma_L^X \Sigma_R^x$, $g_2 = Z_0 Z_{2N} = \Sigma_L^Z \Sigma_R^z$.

### Represent symmetry on one edge
$g_1 = \Sigma_R^x$, $g_2 = \Sigma_R^Z$, anticommute. Projective representation. $\to$ degeneracy.
